## What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. They can be used to remember settings, keep a website working properly, and record consent preferences.

## How This Site Uses Cookies

The site may use cookies or similar browser storage for limited technical purposes such as:

- remembering cookie consent
- supporting core site functionality
- improving reliability and user experience

## Third-Party Services

If analytics, embedded services, or external tools are added in the future, they may also use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

## Managing Cookies

You can usually control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling some storage features may affect how parts of the site work.
